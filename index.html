YUFE Task 5.3

YUFE Task 5.3 Survey

The YUFE Research Relay is a key initiative to foster research collaboration among YUFE universities. Based on valuable data obtained through an internal survey of more than 600 researchers, a program of activities has been designed within work package 5, YUFE Responsible, Interdisciplinary and Inclusive Research (task 5.3: Promoting Research Collaboration) to help researchers create multidisciplinary collaboration groups and devise project proposals that can obtain private and public funding for their implementation.

The first ambitious activity (YUFE Research Relay) proposed to YUFE researchers was specifically designed to help researchers develop competitive, high-quality proposals for Horizon Europe funding. The activity was carried out in three sequential stages: preliminary online sessions, an intensive virtual Ideathon for proposals, and a comprehensive in-person training workshop in Brussels.

These Terms of Reference (ToR) detail the objectives, eligibility criteria, evaluation procedures, and responsibilities of participants. Detailed calls for participation and other specific information can be found in Annex I.
