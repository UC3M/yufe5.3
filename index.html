YUFE Task 5.3

YUFE Research Relay: A Glimpse on Research Fields for Potential Collaboration

The YUFE Research Relay is a key initiative to foster research collaboration among YUFE universities. Based on valuable data obtained through an internal survey of more than 600 researchers carried out in 2024, a program of activities has been designed within work package 5, YUFE Responsible, Interdisciplinary and Inclusive Research (task 5.3: Promoting Research Collaboration) to help researchers create multidisciplinary collaboration groups and devise project proposals that can obtain private and public funding for their implementation.

The first ambitious activity (YUFE Research Relay) proposed to YUFE researchers was specifically designed to help researchers develop competitive, high-quality proposals for Horizon Europe funding. The activity was carried out in three sequential stages: preliminary online sessions, an intensive virtual Ideathon for proposals, and a comprehensive in-person training workshop in Brussels.

This website showcases the results of the survey. Research interests displayed have been extracted from a set of responses to the YUFE Task 5.3 survey. Following the classification available in OpenAlex , the 3 levels displayed are Domain, Field and Subfield. Click on the tabs above to browse the graphs.
